# LinkedIn Headline Optimizer

Free LinkedIn Headline Optimizer for Professionals Across All Industries. Accurate character counter for bold Unicode text, trending SEO keywords for 12+ domains, headline templates, and comprehensive profile health checks. Build compelling headlines in seconds.

## Features

- **12 Professional Domains**: Data & Analytics, Business, Marketing, Engineering, Healthcare, Finance, Sales, HR, Design, Creative, Education, Legal
- **Smart Keywords**: Industry-specific trending keywords to boost LinkedIn SEO
- **Headline Templates**: Pre-built templates for quick inspiration
- **Unicode Styling**: Bold Sans, Bold Serif, Italic Sans, Italic Serif transformations
- **Live Preview**: Real-time preview of your headline
- **Profile Health Checks**: Comprehensive analysis including length, keywords, structure, and ATS compatibility
- **Save/Load**: Local storage to save and reload your headlines
- **Copy to Clipboard**: One-click copying for easy use
- **Responsive Design**: Works perfectly on desktop and mobile

## How to Use

1. Select your professional domain from the tabs
2. Click on keywords to add them to your headline
3. Use templates as starting points
4. Apply styling with the formatting buttons
5. Check the health indicators for optimization tips
6. Save your work and copy when ready
